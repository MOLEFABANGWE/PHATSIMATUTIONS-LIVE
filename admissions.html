Admissions

Payment

Payment must be made in advance before the commencement of tutorial sessions and is due on or before the 1st of every month or the beginning of every term. Payment may be accepted in cash or via bank transfer. Tutorials will be suspended for unpaid dues.

Tuition Fees

  • Non-refundable registration fee: 200 pula
  • PSLE: 1,500 pula per month (4,500 pula per term)
  • JCE: 1,500 pula per month (4,500 pula per term)
  • IGCSE/BGCSE: 1,500 pula per month (4,500 pula per term)
  • Development Levy: 500 pula
  • Siblings discount: 250 pula per student, deductible on the last installment

Cancellation and Refund

Please note that cancellation of service or out-rolling your child midterm will result in no refund. Full term fees are required even during term disturbances (e.g., lockdowns).

Attachments

  • Certified copy of the student's birth certificate
  • Certified copy of the parent's ID
  • Medical report (if necessary)
  • Internal Assessment Report
  • Residence permits for foreigners

NOTE: All outstanding payments overdue by 30 days will be handed to our debt collection department with a 20% recursive interest on the debt.
